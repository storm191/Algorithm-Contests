If you comply with the aforesaid then this is the event for you to show your coding skills. The mind-boggling coding event will challenge not only your programming skills but also your ability to think and the one who will think beyond will win the crown.

Flawless will be the extreme test of every participants coding ability. There will be total two rounds for this event – prelims and finals.

Prelims:

  • Only two problems will be given & the participants will be asked to write codes to solve those.
  • The duration of the round will be 1 hour & 30 minutes.
  • Each team will be provided with only one computer.
  • Marking will be done based on the runtime performance of the programs. 15 top scoring teams will be selected for the final. In case of a tie analysis of the algorithms used will be carried out.

Finals:

  • Five problems will be given.
  • The duration of the round will be 5 hours. 
  • Each team will be provided with two computers each.
  • Marking will be done based on the runtime performance of the programs. Analysis of the algorithm used will be carried out for every solution & each team will be asked to explain the solutions of respective problems to the judges.
  • The programming language used for the event will be C.
  • Each team will consist of at most two members, who can be from different institutions.
  • The participants will not be allowed to use any debugging tools or any kind of electronic devices like pen drive or compact discs.
  • The participants should have a valid ID card of his/her institution.
  • The right spirit of participation is expected from all the participants.
  • The coordinator will have the right to change the rules at any point of time.
