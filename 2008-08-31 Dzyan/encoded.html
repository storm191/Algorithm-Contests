ENCODED

Infosys Sponsored event

              Welcome to Encoded: the mind-boggling coding competition in C. Do you have exceptional programming skills? Can you make computers perform like mere puppets with your codes? Do you want to test your logical and technical skills to solve the innovative problems. If you consider yourself to be a prolific coder this is the platform to take the challenge and show your C-skills. Let Us Code!!

Rules & Regulations :   

1. Each team will consist of at most three students .They will be provided with two computers each.     

2. One member per team has to be registered online.

3. The programming language that will be provided : C.  

4. Use of any  kind of electronic device including  pen drive, compact disc or any memory device is strictly prohibited during the competition.

5. The Coordinators reserve the right to alter any of the rules associated with the event at any  point of time.

6. All students including 2008 passout batch irrespective of their streams can participate in the event.

7. GCC will be provided to compile & execute your code in LINUX platform.

8. Input & Output will be done using command line arguments.

Scoring-pattern:

There will be a Preliminary round and a Final round.

At final there will be  five or six challenging problems of varying difficulty. Marking  will be done on the basis of execution of program at runtime. In case of  any discrepancy, analysis of algorithm will be done subject to the discretion of the coordinators. The decision of the coordinators in these regard will be final.
