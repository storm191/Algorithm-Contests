The Technical festival highlights students' technical skills, creativity as well as their intelligence quotient.

There will be a plethora of events, which includes

'Encoded', a coding competition designed to test the participant’s technical expertise in C.

'RoboHunt', the Robotics competition will lay down a target as the base plan and the participants will have to design a robot in conformance with the provided plan.

'MathMagic', promises to be an exhilarating experience for the mathematical wizards coupled with challenging puzzles and exciting brainteasers.

'Cbug' , the debugging competition in C also deserves a worthy mention.

'Tequizz', tests your knowledge in technical field.

'Sudoku', gives you a challenge to solve it.

Further attractions include 'Battle Royale' a thrilling gaming competition.

Apart from these inter-collegiate events the institute will also host a project competition to showcase the projects of the institute’s students through a Project Demonstration. The project domains include Robotics and its applications .

The winners of the individual events will be handsomely rewarded.
